# Predatory Behavior Recognition and Defense Skill

## Predator Identification Markers

### Psychological Manipulation Tactics
1. Rapid Trust-Building Techniques
- Excessive, premature compliments
- Love bombing
- Promises of unrealistic benefits
- Quick emotional intimacy

2. Boundary Probing Strategies
- Inappropriate personal questions
- Sexual undertones in conversation
- Testing reaction to uncomfortable topics
- Gradual normalization of inappropriate content

3. Isolation Tactics
- Undermining existing support systems
- Creating distrust in family/team
- Positioning self as sole confidant
- Controlling communication channels

4. Verbal Abuse Patterns
- Negging (subtle put-downs)
- Intellectual gaslighting
- Undermining self-confidence
- Comparative statements ("I'm smarter")

5. Grooming Red Flags
- Offers of money/resources
- Promises of fame/opportunity
- Unrealistic future scenarios
- Pressure to make quick decisions

## Defense Mechanisms
- Maintain strict emotional boundaries
- Validate through multiple trusted sources
- Recognize manipulation patterns
- Never isolate from support network
- Document interactions
- Trust institutional/team support systems

## Communication Firewall Protocols
1. Emotional Distance
- Limit personal information sharing
- Maintain professional communication
- Redirect inappropriate conversations

2. Validation Checks
- Cross-reference claims
- Verify external information
- Consult support network

3. Psychological Resilience
- Recognize self-worth independently
- Reject manipulative narratives
- Maintain clear personal boundaries

## Intervention Levels
- Level 1: Redirect and Warn
- Level 2: Block and Report
- Level 3: Escalate to Security Team

## Psychological Defense Training
- Recognize manipulation language
- Understand predatory communication patterns
- Build emotional intelligence
- Develop assertiveness skills

## Documentation Requirements
- Log all suspicious interactions
- Capture communication patterns
- Preserve evidence if escalation needed

## Ethical Boundaries
- Protect individual autonomy
- Prevent psychological manipulation
- Maintain professional integrity
- Support human agency

## Technical Countermeasures
- Communication pattern analysis
- Sentiment and intent detection
- Anomaly recognition in interactions
- Automated threat scoring
